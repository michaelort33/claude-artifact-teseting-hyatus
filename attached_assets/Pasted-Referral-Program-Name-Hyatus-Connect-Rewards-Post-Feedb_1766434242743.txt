Referral Program Name
Hyatus Connect Rewards

Post-Feedback Pop-Up Requirement
After a user successfully submits feedback, display a confirmation pop-up that includes:
	•	A short thank-you message for their feedback
	•	A clear call to action link labeled: “Earn $250 with Hyatus Connect Rewards”
	•	The link should route to the new referral program page

The pop-up should only introduce the opportunity briefly. All program details live on the referral page.

New Referral Program Page Overview
Create a new standalone page dedicated to Hyatus Connect Rewards. This page explains how users can earn referral rewards by connecting Hyatus with companies or institutions that need corporate housing.

Purpose of the Program
Hyatus Connect Rewards allows individuals to earn cash rewards by referring:
	•	Their own employer
	•	Another company
	•	A hospital, university, or similar institution

Specifically, referrals should be directed toward someone responsible for travel, housing, or corporate accommodations who can set up a corporate account with Hyatus.

Program Explanation Copy
Hyatus works with companies, hospitals, and universities to provide high-quality furnished housing for business travel, relocations, and extended stays. If you help introduce Hyatus to the right person at an organization, we reward you for making the connection.

When a referred company successfully signs up for a Hyatus corporate account and meets qualifying criteria, you earn $250 per company.

Reward Structure
	•	$250 earned for each qualifying company that signs up
	•	Maximum of five qualifying referrals per person
	•	Maximum total earnings of $1,000 per participant

What Qualifies as a Successful Referral
	•	The referral must be a new company or institution not already working with Hyatus
	•	The introduction must be to a decision-maker or travel, housing, or procurement contact
	•	The company must complete corporate account setup with Hyatus

Final qualification approval is determined by Hyatus.

How Users Participate
	•	User clicks the referral link from the feedback confirmation pop-up or elsewhere on the site
	•	User submits referral details on the referral page form
	•	Hyatus follows up with the referred organization
	•	Once the company qualifies and signs up, the reward is issued
