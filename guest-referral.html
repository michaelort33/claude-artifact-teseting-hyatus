How It Works

Know someone who needs furnished housing for work, relocation, or an extended stay? Refer them to Hyatus, and when they book, you'll earn a reward for helping them find their perfect home away from home.

What Qualifies as a Successful Referral?

  • The guest must be new to Hyatus (not an existing guest)
  • Your friend must complete a qualifying stay (minimum 30 nights)
  • Final qualification approval is determined by Hyatus
